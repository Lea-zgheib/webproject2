Middle East Internet Adoption & Usage

  • High Internet Penetration: The Middle East boasts internet penetration rates exceeding 90%, with countries like the UAE and Qatar leading the region.
  • Mobile Dominance: A significant portion of internet traffic in the Middle East originates from mobile devices, emphasizing the importance of mobile-first design.
  • Social Media Engagement: Social media usage is prevalent, with platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter being widely used across the region.
  • Digital Divide: While urban areas have robust internet access, rural regions may experience slower speeds and limited connectivity.

Lebanon Internet Statistics

  • Internet Users: As of early 2025, Lebanon had 5.34 million internet users, representing 91.6% of the population.
  • Mobile Internet Speeds: The median mobile internet speed was 31.06 Mbps, reflecting improvements in mobile infrastructure.
  • Fixed Internet Speeds: Fixed broadband speeds averaged 9.39 Mbps, indicating ongoing efforts to enhance fixed-line internet services.
